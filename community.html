Volunteer Your Time & Skill

GIVE YOUR TIME & TALENT

We at KCW value your time and skills deeply. Your time is irreplaceable — and therefore of immeasurable value to us. Do you have an hour, or several hours a week? You can direct that time towards creating positive change in Kenya.

Write to us and we shall guide you on how to engage. If you have a skill you would like to volunteer, you are equally welcome — every talent finds a home at KCW.

IFK/B4 Conferences

BUY YOUR CONFERENCE TICKET

KCW runs 49 different IFK/B4 conferences annually across Kenya — 47 county conferences and 2 national events. Find a conference near you and buy your ticket online. These conferences provide a unique opportunity to network, partner, and organise for change.

Dates and venues for county conferences will be published progressively throughout the year. Check back regularly or contact us to confirm schedules in your county.
